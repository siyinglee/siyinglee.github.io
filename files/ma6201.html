Introduction to Shimura varieties

Lecture: Thursday 10am-1pm.

The aim of this course is to introduce the theory of Shimura varieties, and related topics. Key topics include: Abelian varieties and p-divisible groups, definition and classification of Shimura data, canonical models, integral models, zeta functions of Shimura varieties.

Students taking the course for a grade will be required to give an in-class presentation (40%) and write a short final paper (5-10 pages, 60%) on a chosen topic. Undergraduate students interested should contact me for approval to enroll.
